NYC Police Precinct Residents Serving Prison Sentence

This visualization shows the number of residents per 100,000 from each New York City police precinct that are currently a sentence in New York State prison. Clear patterns emerge from this data where precincts that are very high and those that are very low tend to be clustered near each other.

NYC Police Precinct Population Density vs. Incarceration

This map shows that there tend to be clusters of police precints with similar values, such as lower Manhatten, which has very high density and very low incarceration. There map indicates that there is not a clear correlation between the two variables as there is a fairly even distribution of the different quadrants.

Brown Co, OH Educational Attainment

This map indicates that there are significant differences in educational attainment based on different areas of the county. There is a not a clear spatial pattern for the census tract areas with higher or lower education attainment.

Housing in Ohio

This is an interactive web application that allows users to compare visualizations relating to housing demographics in Ohio at the county level. Quantile, equal interval, and jenks natural breaks classifications are available. Spatial patterns emerge strongly in some visualizations, while in others there is no discernable pattern of the spatial distribution of data. Some moderately strongly relationships emerge when comparing some of the variables.

Iowa City Trees Interactive Map

This interactive web map allows users to explore the data on 20,000+ trees in Iowa City that were captured in a survey from 2019. Two of the major predictors of risk to personal property in the event of a major storm, condition and diameter at breast height are captured through point color and point size respectively.
