William Cooper UX Designer

Hi there! I am a second year student at the University of Iowa studying Library and Information Science with an emphasis on informatics. My main areas of interest are UX research, UX design, and information architecture. I'm passionate about improving information systems to better meet the needs of the humans (not just users) who might interact with them.

I currently work as a UX designer for N3C (National COVID Cohort Collaborative), the nation's largest repository for clinical data relatign to COVID-19. This past year I have also been collaborating with the University of Colorado Boulder to lead a UX research project focused on evaluating the usability of the CU Boulder GeoLibrary, a geospatial data repository.
